PRODUCTION ROOM

For Producers who use Laptop, Tablet or a Mobile Device as an audio source, E.A.E Recording Studio provides a Production Room which is dedicated to transform your Portable Music Production Device into a professional studio by connecting it to our Production Room.

We connect your device to our dual monitors with large display & touchscreen display monitor along with 3D Sound studio monitors & sub-woofer supported by sound-proofed walls & wireless connectivity so that you can hear the true sound of your mix in the Production Room.

The purpose of this room is for Producing, Composing, Editing, Arranging & Mixing on a Digital Audio Workstation (D.A.W.) operated by you. All you have to do is bring your Portable Music Production Device and we connect it to our Production Room so that you can work your magic by utilising hi-end tech & the power of E.A.E.

JAM ROOM

For Musicians who need a place to rehearse, practice or record their talent, E.A.E Recording Studio provides a Jam Room dedicated for musicians who use musical instruments as their audio source.

The Jam Room provides musicians with musical instruments such as:

Keyboard, Electronic Drums, Xylophone, Tabla, Damru, Shakers, Violin, Electronic Guitar, Electronic Bass Guitar, Semi-Acoustic Guitar, Acoustic Guitar & Classical Guitar.

All Musical Instruments provided by E.A.E Recording Studio in the Jam Room are tuned & output high quality audio via high quality audio cables.

Hear yourself play loud & clear as we provide musicians with either noise cancelling headphones or wireless headphones, if needed.


RECORDING ROOM

E.A.E Recording Studio provides a Recording Room which is equipped with hi-end audio devices such as:

Graphic Equalizers, Pre-Amps, Audio Interface, Effect Processors, Voice Transformer, Multi-Channel Digital Mixing Console, World-class Studio Monitors & Sub-woofer, Powerful Computer with fully loaded D.A.W, Multiple Displays & Touchscreen Interface. All audio devices in the Recording Room output high quality audio via high quality audio cables.

The purpose of this room is for Recording, Editing, Audio Processing, Effect Processing, Mixing & Mastering on a D.A.W operated by our Recording Engineer.

In this Room, audio tracks will be arranged, finalized & ready to export. All you have to do is Instruct, Guide or Mention your preferences to our Recording Engineer, who will make your track sound the way you prefer.

STUDIO CLOUD

E.A.E Studio Cloud feature lets our client send or receive files & data and can be used as a medium to transfer Data such as Audio, Project File, PDF, Document File to & from the studio directly to or from your laptop, tablet or mobile device without any size restriction. You can Register to our Client’s Account for this privilege.
